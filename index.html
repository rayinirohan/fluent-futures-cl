How many colleges accept IELTS test scores?

The IELTS exam is by far the most popular English proficiency test accepted by institutions across the world. Thousands of colleges and universities mandate this test for students coming from countries such as India in order to ensure they have the required English proficiency to succeed in a college environment.

Why is scoring 6+ bands on the IELTS test important?

Just taking the IELTS exam isn’t enough. Each university requires its applicants to score a minimum band to be eligible for its programs. Students who score less than the prescribed IELTS band score in even one section stand to lose their provisional admission offer. That’s why it is so important to score 6+ bands overall and in all sections of the IELTS test to ensure that you meet the minimum English proficiency benchmarks for all programs across all universities around the world and can have a hassle-free admission process to your preferred university. This is also why most experts recommend taking IELTS coaching from experienced mentors who can guide you to the required 6+ band score.
